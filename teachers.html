Questions teachers ask

Which exam boards does it cover?
Primarily AMEB for now. Since Grade 1–3 theory content is largely universal, most content is also relevant for ABRSM, RCM, and Trinity students. Board-specific modules are planned for later.
Can I see my students' progress?
Not yet — progress is currently stored on each student's device. A teacher dashboard is on the roadmap. For now, the Daily Challenge streak and module high scores give students (and you) a clear picture.
Does it work without internet?
The app is a PWA and installs as a home screen icon. Static assets are cached for offline use. The piano audio samples require an initial download (~10MB) but are then cached.
Is Grade 1 really free forever?
Yes — no credit card, no trial period, no account required for Grade 1. Students and parents can use it indefinitely at no cost.
What devices does it work on?
Any modern browser: iPad (Safari), iPhone, Android phone/tablet, Mac, Windows PC. Designed first for tablet, fully responsive on all screen sizes.
How do I refer multiple students? Is there a class code?
Not yet, but teacher referral codes are coming. For now, sharing the URL (music.vensoai.com) is all that's needed — Grade 1 requires no account, and the paid upgrade is simple for parents.
